02
OVERVIEW

GOAL

The goal of this project is to create the new design identity for the brand to help them establish in the market and create a memorable and lasting impression..

BACKGROUND

Dime is a interior design studio that offers various range of services to people and help them turn their houses to home. The brand is focused on minimalistic, clean and professional look and they want clients to trust them and their professionalism. It didn’t have any brand identity initial, since Dime entered the market 3 month ago and was in need of a website.

03
STRATEGY

Target Audience

Dime's target audience encompasses a diverse range of individuals, predominantly consisting of homeowners, young professionals, and urban dwellers in the age range of 25 to 45. This demographic values contemporary design trends and seeks to infuse their living spaces with a sense of simplicity, functionality, and elegance. They are digitally savvy and often turn to online platforms for inspiration and guidance in their interior design endeavours.

Values

•Professionalism: Dime upholds the highest standards of professionalism, fostering transparent communication, reliability, and trustworthiness throughout every client interaction. •Creativity: Dime embraces boundless creativity, crafting innovative design concepts tailored to each client's unique preferences and needs, ensuring that every project is a one-of-a-kind masterpiece. •Collaboration: Dime values collaboration, fostering open dialogue and teamwork between clients and designers to bring their visions to life, resulting in spaces that truly reflect the client's personality and lifestyle.

Tone of voice / Message

Where minimalist design meets personalized style, creating inviting spaces you'll love coming home to

04
DESIGN PROCESS
OUTCOME

I’ve created a new logo in minimalistic style that showcases building outline with their brand name at bottom as well as brand guideline that will help the brand stay cohesive in the future across all platforms. After establishing their brand identity, it was time to design their website layout that will also follow the minimalistic, clean creative direction with interesting grid and use of white space. The UI/UX of the website is simple yet effective that captures their target audience’s attention perfectly and attract them even more.
