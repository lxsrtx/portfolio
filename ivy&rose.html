IVY & ROSE

01

Brand Identity

02 | OVERVIEW

Goal

The goal is to create a brand identity for a jewelry brand that embodies both sophistication and bravery.

Background

Ivy&Rose is a high-end jewelry brand renowned for its exquisite craftsmanship and timeless designs.Ivy&Rose entered the market a year ago. Their company sought to serve clients by being innovative and brining the best products to our clients. Currently, the brand wants to widen their target audience to whole Europe and North America as well as cover younger generation.

03 | STRATEGY

Target Audience

Our brand specializes in classic jewelry pieces with bold, edgy elements for people, who wants to experiment with their style. For women 25-40 who are lavish and detail oriented our product is timeless jewelry that is crafted with high quality and nontraditional designs.

Brand Values

• Transparency: We are completely honest with our clients about our work ethic, process and what brand we actually are.
• Authenticity: We make handmade, man-crafted jewelry with original, unique designs. We stand for authentic design.
• Bravery: We are not afraid to create bold design choices and we face challenges to inspire others.

Tone of voice / Message

Ivy&Rose's message is to make you feel special and unique.

04 | Design Process

Ivy&Rose was a project that I created in my second year of education and I had mixed feelings about it upon finishing. After gaining more knowledge and skills, I went back to the same project and redesigned it with better skillset, better design vision and I’m in love how it turned out. To convey the feeling of timeless I decided to choose serif font that reflects elegance and sophistication. The colour palette consists of rich jewel tones, reminiscent of luxury and opulence. The logo, now refined with sleek lines and intricate detailing, serves as a hallmark of the brand's craftsmanship and attention to detail. The layout design is carefully curated, with a harmonious balance of negative space and visual elements, guiding the viewer's eye effortlessly. Updated their logo and created a new brand guidelines that will help the brand to be cohesive across all platforms. Packaging and their promotional pieces follows the guideline and targeted towards their new audience that they established earlier.
