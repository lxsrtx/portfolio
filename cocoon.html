Brand Identity

02 | OVERVIEW

Goal

The goal of this project is to improve their design strategy and identity of the brand to help them expand their sale volume and attract new clients.

Background

Cocoon is a brand that focuses on blankets. Cocoon’s design style is a harmonious blend of modern sophistication and natural warmth. The brand aims to create an inviting atmosphere that resonates with comfort and coziness. It’s fairly new to the market by being a year old brand. Their struggle is making sales and attracting clients, which makes their brand strategy ineffective.

03 | STRATEGY

Target Audience

Cocoon's target audience consists of comfort seekers aged 14 to 30, residing in urban and suburban areas. They prioritize relaxation and self-care in their busy lives, valuing quality, comfort, and sustainability.

Brand Values

• Comfort: Cocoon is committed to providing unparalleled comfort to its customers, ensuring that every product contributes to a sense of relaxation and well-being.
• Quality: Cocoon prioritizes quality craftsmanship and materials in every aspect of its blankets, ensuring durability, longevity, and customer satisfaction.
• Sustainability: Cocoon is dedicated to environmental responsibility, sourcing eco-friendly materials, minimizing waste, and supporting sustainable manufacturing practices.

Tone of voice / Message

Wrap yourself in warmth and create your cocoon of comfort with Cocoon.

04 | Design Process

Based on the strategy that was set previously, I redesigned the old logo and established new colour palette with more vibrant colours and typography that conveys bubbly, friendly feeling, since their target audience is focused on people from 14 to 30 years old with fun and outgoing personality. Brand guidelines were created with their new brand strategy that will help with staying cohesive and worked on 2D packaging sleeve design with different colour variation for different type of blankets. The new brand identity will be the main selling point for Cocoon since it better targets their audience. Overall, I enjoyed working on this project, since it meant for me to get out of my comfort zone and try different design style.
