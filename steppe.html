Editorial Design + Issue Development

02 | OVERVIEW

Goal

The goal of this project is to create a new magazine brand and develop it's creative direction with own style, typography and graphic elements that covers profile story, cover story, mini features.

Background

This project took three months to complete. The task involved creating magazine identity that speaks for me and deciding on direction that it will take. I decided to go with Central Asia focused magazine that will cover each country each issue every year. The project delved into creating new font, magazine layout, imagery styles, hierarchy, typography.

03 | STRATEGY

Name of publication

Steppe is a magazine about Central Asia countries' culture, history and heritage. It promotes cultural appreciation, historical awareness and cross-cultural understanding. It will be published once a year covering different countries and it will cost 10$ per issue. The magazine will adopt a narrative driven approach for its features. Each article could unfold as a story, taking readers on a journey through the cultural, historical, and contemporary landscapes of Central Asia’s 1 country each issue.

Target Audience

The target audience are young adults and millennials as well as middle aged and older adults with deeper appreciation for history, cultural traditions. My magazine for global audience, who are cultural enthusiasts, adventure seekers and history buffs with curious and open-minded personality. Age will range from 22 to 60 years old, both gender and located mostly in urban area with high education. They are middle/upper middle class and their life stage may vary from students, young professionals to families and retirees.

Tone of voice / Message

My magazine holds educational value offering readers insights into rich history, diverse culture and unique heritage of Central Asia countries. It will also raise cultural awareness with deeper understanding of the traditions and languages. It might also inspire some readers to explore more Central Asia countries and diverse themself in their culture. Steppe will use storytelling techniques to bring Central Asian stories to life. Craft compelling narratives that captivate readers and make them feel connected to the people, places, and histories it features.

04 | Design Process

I think the Vogue magazine project turned out really well. I focused on keeping everything connected across the 40 pages. By using repeated elements, like those solid rectangles, I made sure the design flowed smoothly. I chose a golden yellow color that wasn't too flashy but added a classy touch, keeping things calm and elegant. Using mostly one color made the different colors used for features stand out and kept things interesting. The layout, with lots of white space and some breaks from the usual order, helped keep readers engaged. Overall, the Vogue project is a cohesive and visually appealing piece that I'm proud of.
