Brand Identity + Web Development

02 | OVERVIEW

Goal

The goal of this project is to create a skincare brand that will differ from others with its vibrant colors and fun approach. The project will cover logo creation, brand guidelines, packaging design and website layout, both web and mobile.

Background

Our skincare brand is a vibrant celebration of individuality. We are a skincare routine that mirrors your spirit - joyful, funny and unhinged. We're not just about skincare; were about embracing your individuality and expressing yourself in the most fun and quirky ways possible. At Ozge, we believe in breaking the rules and celebrating the beauty of being unapologetically yourself.

03 | STRATEGY

Target Audience

It is composed of individuals aged 18 to 35, residing in urban and trendy neighborhoods. They embody playful, adventurous, and socially engaged personalities, with a strong creative streak. These individuals are eager to experiment with beauty and skincare products, constantly on the lookout for the latest trends to try and share with their social media followers. They are active participants in online beauty communities, often influenced by what’s popular on platforms like Instagram and TikTok. They appreciate brands that incorporate humor and playfulness into their marketing, enjoying campaigns that are light-hearted and don't take themselves too seriously. Ozge appeals to this audience by aligning with their values and lifestyle, offering products that are not only effective but also fun and trendy, making skincare an exciting and enjoyable part of their daily routine.

Brand Values

• Creativity: Encourage self-expression and individuality, offering products that allow customers to experiment and create their unique beauty routines.
• Inclusivity: Celebrate diversity and ensure that the brand is welcoming to all, offering products that cater to a wide range of skin types, tones, and concerns.
• Authenticity: Maintain transparency in product ingredients, sourcing, and brand messaging, fostering trust and loyalty among customers.

Tone of voice / Message

To creates tools for self-expression and bring people together.

04 | Design Process

One of the projects I'm most proud of is the magazine identity I created. Initially, I was afraid that I wouldn't be able to achieve the vision I had for it. It was a bit challenging to incorporate ornamental elements as page furniture without making the pages visually heavy. For each issue, I plan to use unique ornaments and national colors from different countries as the pages progress. The Kazakhstan issue, for example, has its own flow with red, beige, and green colors. You can see how the red is present in the stories about our strong, brave Kazakh women who have achieved so much for our people, while the beige is used in stories about the auls and the nomadic lifestyle that Kazakhs had in the past. The carefully selected imagery, with its special effect of old, shabby pictures, also helps readers immerse themselves in the stories of my people and their history. I'm proud of my heritage, and I will continue to share it with others.
